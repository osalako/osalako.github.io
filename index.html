About Me

As a leading analytics project manager...

I build statistical models and design enterprise-wide business intelligence solutions to drive strategy & development of cross-functional teams, improve key performance metrics, and eliminate inconsistencies. My inquisitive and collaborative nature stems from a variety of my personal and professional experiences in data visualization, predictive analytics, executive communications, media production, and talent development.

When I cultivate a growth mindset and remain open to possibilities, I find myself developing in better ways than I ever imagined. This has led me to pursue a Master of Science in Analytics from Georgia Institute of Technology – building on my undergraduate career in economics and management information systems, as well as strengthening my skills in big data computing and machine learning.

My ability to identify opportunities, assemble buy-in, organize resources for execution, collaborate with developer teams, and build problem-solving tools has contributed to my most recent professional successes. These include sharpening selling and marketing strategies with segmentation modeling, enhancing classification algorithms to increase accuracy, improvements in staffing effectiveness, transparency in analyzing cost trends, and a business cultural transformation in a department with 900+ employees across the nation.

As a Los Angeles native, I thrive best in diverse and high-demand environments. I enjoy travelling to new cities and binge-watching a great series. I currently reside in Dallas, Texas where I work as a data scientist collaborating with others on modeling and statistical analysis to help drive revenue growth and provide marketing insights for the AT&T Business organization.

Houston Neighborhood Quality Dashboard

As part of a Data Visual and Analytics group project, we created a user-friendly, interactive dashboard explaining non-emergency 311 data in the city of Houston. Our novel approach was using Principal Component Analysis on population-weighted data to calculate a neighborhood quality rating. Trial users rated the dashboard 4.5 out of 5.

Volunteer

The NETwork is an organization whose vision is to become a premier leader in providing growth opportunities through sustainable programs that build future generational leaders within the industry and community. With a 50-year heritage, the NETwork is AT&T’s oldest employee resource group.

I am currently serving as the National Mentoring Program Director, since July 2020. Working closely with the Talent Development Officer, our aim is to transform the development experience of NETwork members through targeted mentoring from leaders across the business – enriching both their professional and personal lives.

We organized and launched a new mentoring program from the ground up that formally began in January 2021. This program includes a full year of mentoring where employees are matched with leaders for a mentorship experience centered around adaptability, mentoring/sponsorship/advocacy, building relationships, and continuous learning. Our mentors range from high performing managers all the way to Senior Vice President and other Officer roles throughout all business units in the company.

In our inaugural year, we had about 100 mentors providing guidance and advice to over 400 mentees; this includes 60+ mentoring circles and 30+ one-on-one mentorships. There is true value in having leaders passionate about the development and growth of others. I look forward to pioneering similar programs in the future.
